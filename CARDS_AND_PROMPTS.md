# Card Artwork Prompts

## Style Suffix (shared by all cards)

```
Dramatic close-up scene in deep space, painterly sci-fi illustration style, vertical portrait composition, rich colors with strong contrast, atmospheric lighting, no text, no UI elements, no characters, no card frames, 560x880
```

## Type Base Prompts

### ATTACK (type 0)
```
Weapons firing and energy beams in space combat, aggressive red and orange energy tones, explosive impact effects, sense of destructive power
```

### DEFENSE (type 1)
```
Protective shields and barriers activating in space, cool blue and cyan energy tones, glowing defensive forcefields, sense of protection and resilience
```

### UTILITY (type 2)
```
Ship systems and technology in space, green and teal circuit-like energy tones, mechanical components and repair drones, sense of clever engineering
```

### TRADE (type 3)
```
Cargo and commerce in space, warm gold and amber tones, valuable materials and containers, sense of wealth and opportunity
```
